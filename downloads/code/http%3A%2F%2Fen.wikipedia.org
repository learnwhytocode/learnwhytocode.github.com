<div id="mp-tfa" style="padding:2px 5px">
<div style="float:left;margin:0.5em 0.9em 0.4em 0;"><a href="/wiki/File:Signature_of_Chrisye.svg" class="image" title="Signature of Chrisye"><img alt="Signature of Chrisye" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/3/3d/Signature_of_Chrisye.svg/130px-Signature_of_Chrisye.svg.png" width="130" height="68" /></a></div>
<p><b><a href="/wiki/Chrisye" title="Chrisye">Chrisye</a></b> (1949–2007) was a <a href="/wiki/Chinese-Indonesian" title="Chinese-Indonesian" class="mw-redirect">Chinese-Indonesian</a> singer and songwriter. Born in <a href="/wiki/Jakarta" title="Jakarta">Jakarta</a>, he became interested in music as a teenager. After joining a band in the 1960s and playing in New York, in 1976 he and the band worked on a collaboration with <a href="/wiki/Guruh_Sukarnoputra" title="Guruh Sukarnoputra">Guruh Sukarnoputra</a>. The success of <a href="/wiki/Guruh_Gipsy" title="Guruh Gipsy">this album</a> led to Chrisye being approached for two projects: the song "<a href="/wiki/Lilin-Lilin_Kecil" title="Lilin-Lilin Kecil">Lilin-Lilin Kecil</a>" ("Small Candles") and the album <i><a href="/wiki/Badai_Pasti_Berlalu_(album)" title="Badai Pasti Berlalu (album)">Badai Pasti Berlalu</a></i> (<i>The Storm Will Surely Pass</i>). Both projects were highly successful, and as a result Chrisye was signed to <a href="/wiki/Musica_Studios" title="Musica Studios">Musica Studios</a>. With them, he released nineteen <a href="/wiki/Studio_albums" title="Studio albums" class="mw-redirect">studio albums</a> before his death from lung cancer. Chrisye was known as a stiff stage performer but widely praised for his vocal abilities. <i><a href="/wiki/Rolling_Stone_Indonesia" title="Rolling Stone Indonesia" class="mw-redirect">Rolling Stone Indonesia</a></i> listed <a href="/wiki/Chrisye_discography" title="Chrisye discography">five of his albums</a> and <a href="/wiki/List_of_Chrisye_songs" title="List of Chrisye songs">four of his songs</a> as among the best in Indonesian music history; the magazine selected Chrisye as the third-best Indonesian musical artist of all time. His albums were mostly successful, and almost all were <a href="/wiki/Music_recording_sales_certification" title="Music recording sales certification">certified</a> silver or higher. <b>(<a href="/wiki/Chrisye" title="Chrisye">more...</a>)</b></p>

<li style="-moz-float-edge: content-box"><a href="/wiki/1776" title="1776">1776</a> – <a href="/wiki/American_Revolutionary_War" title="American Revolutionary War">American Revolutionary War</a>: On hearing the British troops sounding their <a href="/wiki/Bugle_(instrument)" title="Bugle (instrument)" class="mw-redirect">bugles</a> as if it were a <a href="/wiki/Fox_hunting" title="Fox hunting">fox hunt</a>, the American colonists held their ground and achieved a victory at the <b><a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Harlem_Heights" title="Battle of Harlem Heights">Battle of Harlem Heights</a></b> in present-day <a href="/wiki/New_York_City" title="New York City">New York City</a>.</li>

<li style="-moz-float-edge: content-box"><a href="/wiki/1982" title="1982">1982</a> – A Lebanese militia under the direct command of <a href="/wiki/Elie_Hobeika" title="Elie Hobeika">Elie Hobeika</a> carried out a massacre in the <a href="/wiki/Palestinian_people" title="Palestinian people">Palestinian</a> refugee camp of <b><a href="/wiki/Sabra_and_Shatila_massacre" title="Sabra and Shatila massacre">Sabra and Shatila</a></b>, killing at least 700 civilians.</li>
<li style="-moz-float-edge: content-box"><a href="/wiki/2007" title="2007">2007</a> – Seventeen Iraqi civilians were <b><a href="/wiki/Blackwater_Baghdad_shootings" title="Blackwater Baghdad shootings">shot and killed</a></b>, allegedly by <a href="/wiki/Xe_Services" title="Xe Services" class="mw-redirect">Blackwater Worldwide</a> mercenaries; all criminal charges were later dropped.</li>
